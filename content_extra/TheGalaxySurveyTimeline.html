The galaxy redshift surveys

In this document I provide the historical review of galaxy surveys. It descript the charateristics of each galaxy survey. This is useful for comparing how each survey suits scientific studies of astrophysics and cosmology.

Below shows the timeline of when each survey was carried out (or is planed) for observation. Many of the past surveys has set the stepping stone for ongoing and upcoming survey. They have shaped the understanding of the evolution of the Universe and how galaxies formed at different cosmic epochs.
